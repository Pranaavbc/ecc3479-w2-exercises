quarterly labour market statistics for Australian states in 2022

Source: Australian Bureau of Statistics – Labour Force Survey
Accessed: 10 March 2026

Version: 1

Variables: 

Variable	Description
state - Australian state where the observation was recorded (e.g., NSW, VIC, QLD).

quarter - The quarter of the year when the observation was recorded (Q1–Q4).

year - Calendar year of the observation (2022).

unemployment_rate - Percentage of the labour force that is unemployed but actively seeking work.

participation_rate - Percentage of the working-age population participating in the labour force (either employed or actively seeking work).

Limitations: 
- only covers three Australian states and one year (2022), cannot find future trends. 
- estimates may contain sampling error 
- short term fluctuations may be masked by quarterly aggregation
- dataset may be simplified
